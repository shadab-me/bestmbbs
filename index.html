Documents Required for Admission

  • The medical aspirants need to present the form with proper signature along with all correct information.
  • The parents must submit a form of undertaking to the university.
  • First years tuition fee bank receipt.
  • Passing certificate from the previous school/college.
  • Copies of academic certificates.
  • Valid caste certificate to verify the pass percentage.
  • Scorecard of NEET entrance test.
  • Recent passport size photograph.
  • Valid birth certificate.
  • Medical report with negative HIV test report.
